About Rise Dishonored

**Rise Dishonored** is an action-adventure game crafted for those with an eye for grand knightly adventures and honorable battles. Prepare yourself for a life steeped in the valor of knights and the majesty of kingdoms; fight for steel and blood. Prove yourself trustworthy and carve your legend.

Game Details

  • **Genre:** Adventure, Indie
  • **Developer:** Risetopia, Ra's Vision
  • **Publisher:** Krill Studio
  • **Player Modes:** Single-player, PvP (Online), PvP (Shared/Split Screen), Co-op (Online), Co-op (Shared/Split Screen)
  • **Features:** Cross-Platform Multiplayer, Steam Achievements, In-App Purchases, Steam Cloud, Family Sharing
  • **Controller Support:** Full controller support (Xbox, PlayStation, Steam Input API)
  • **Anti-Cheat:** Utilizes OS kernel-level anti-cheat (BattlEye)
  • **Languages:**
    • **Interface:** Bulgarian, English, Russian, Spanish - Spain
    • **Audio:** Bulgarian, English, Russian, Spanish - Spain
    • **Subtitles:** English, Spanish - Spain
  • **Content Warning:** Contains Violence, Alcohol, Nudity, Blood, Strong Language, Online Play
  • **Interactive Elements:** In-game Purchases, In-game Chat
